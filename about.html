About Fuzzlab Interactive

Fuzzlab ALPHA was born on the 22nd of February, 2001 at 5:06pm. While the concept of Fuzzlab has been around for years, only recently did i realize its true vision: Creativity through chaos - beauty within the noise. Fuzzlab is more than a music studio, it's a complete creative studio, utilizing any means necessary to create. What others call trash, i see as raw materials for new beginnings. Fuzzlab.net was born on Fool's Day, 2001 - an unexpected twin of Fuzzlab ALPHA and an online extension of the Fuzzlab concept. For years it has been a goal of mine to provide a space where myself and other artists could showcase our work without advertising or hosting fees. A place where artists of all types could communicate with each other and share ideas. These concepts have come to life in fuzzlab.net. Over time this Web site will grow to accomodate audio/video streaming, message boards, artist shopping carts, an online bartering system, and much, much more. Enjoy your tour of fuzzlab.net, and come back to see how we've grown. ~hed fuzz
